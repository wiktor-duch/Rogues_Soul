INSTRUCTION:
Play the game 5-10 times and send the statistics printed at the end of each game to Wiktor Duch.

GAMR LAUNCHING:
Run 'main.py' with Python 3.

HOW TO PLAY:
At the bottom of each frame you have an input area. Type one of the letters below and press [ENTER].
Available letters:
    - 'a'/'A' - left
    - 'd'/'D' - right
    - 'w'/'W' - up
    - 's'/'S' - down
    - 'p'/'P' - print your equipment
    - 'q'/'Q' - quit
